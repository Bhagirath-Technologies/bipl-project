Corporate Profile

Bhagirath Industries Private Limited, founded in 2019 and headquartered in Ahmedabad Gujarat India, is emerging Agri Business of India the only fully Integrated Agro Processing, Edible Oil Refining, Bio-Diesel, Oleochemical and Bio - Polymer Producing Company with Zero Waste & Zero Discharge Unit. Bhagirath Industries is committed to provide significant contribution to the Green Environment, Circular Sustainable Growth, Sustainable Ecosystem and Green Economy, with Self Sustainability specifically into Plantation Oil Palm Trees, Agriculture (Oil Seed, Sugar & Grains Farming), Sugar Milling Refining, Crushing, Milling, Edible Oil Processing, Starch Manufacturing, Lactic Acid, Poly Lactic Acid, Bio-Polymers, Bio-Diesel and Oleochemicals & Bio-Based Products in totality. Founder Team comprises of strong “bottom-up” thinkers and “sleeves rolled up” operators, having wealth of experience and Expertise into Techno Commercial and operating sector in the field of the Energy, Power, Oil & Gas, Polymer and Food Processing & Manufacturing Sector. At the core Bhagirath‟s strategy is an integrated Agri Business Model that encompasses the entire value chain of the agricultural commodity business, from cultivation, processing, merchandising to manufacturing of a wide range of agricultural products.

OUR GLOBAL AIM

Reinterpret global agriculture and food systems to provide food, feed, and fiber that's better for farmers, communities, and the environment. We aim to create sustainable systems that support healthier ecosystems and communities while improving global agricultural practices overall.

OUR AIM FOR INDIA

Reinterpret and implement agriculture systems that make India self-sufficient, minimizing reliance on edible and essential oil imports while promoting economic sustainability. Our focus is on building a strong value chain for byproducts, supporting industries, and enhancing overall food and agricultural resilience.

Our people are our greatest competitive advantage. They shape our future by transforming our culture into a powerful force driving business growth globally. Across products, regions, and functions, this shared philosophy strengthens our capabilities, empowering us to create innovative solutions everywhere we serve.

Partners & Growing Responsibility

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur. Placerat ac eu at tincidunt at.

The drivers of deforestation are complex global forces that are beyond the power of any one industry or company to tackle alone. Collective endeavour by businesses, governments, communities and civil society is required to effect real and lasting change on the ground. Bhagirath Industries is active in partnerships and collaborations to achieve transformation at the landscape level. Agriculture faces some of the world's biggest development, economic, and environmental challenges. To reinterpret global agriculture we need to harness technology, the vast experience of colleagues and the expertise of those outside our organisation. We encourage entrepreneurship and partner with us. We extend our experience and financial assistance as helping hand to make partners owners of their respective field and become our business partner by our extended collaboration to become supplier or vendor partner adding value to value chain for common goal.

What encourage us to establish and develop Partners?
  • We dare to dream.
  • Our passion for doing more
  • We strive to develop positive and long-term relationships with our partners
  • We stay true to what we believe, say and do
  • We take responsibility as if we were founders of the business.

Conducting our business in an ethical, socially responsible and environmentally sustainable manner is part of our core strategy. Growing Responsibly doesn't just mean protecting the environment and supporting farmers and communities associated. There are clear commercial factors, such as having a sound business model with strong risk management and governance, so that we protect our investors, shareholders, stakeholders, partners and employees, which in turn mean we have a resilient and sustainable business for our farmers, suppliers, partners and customers.
