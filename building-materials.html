PRODUCTS WE SERVE

Discover top-quality polymers at Bhagirath Industry. As leading suppliers, we provide a wide range of innovative polymer solutions. Our commitment to excellence ensures industries receive premium-grade materials, promoting efficiency and sustainability. Partner with us for all your polymer needs, driving progress through advanced chemical solutions.

STEEL

  • Widely used in construction, automotive, and manufacturing industries.
  • Offers high tensile strength and corrosion resistance.
  • Available in various grades and types, such as stainless and carbon steel.
  • Produced through processes like Basic Oxygen Furnace (BOF) and Electric Arc Furnace (EAF).
  • Recyclable, making it a sustainable choice for modern industries.

CEMENT

  • Essential ingredient in concrete and mortar production.
  • Provides durability and strength to buildings and infrastructure.
  • Available in various types, such as Portland, white, and blended cement.
  • Produced through a process of crushing, heating, and grinding raw materials.
  • Requires careful curing to achieve optimal hardness and longevity.

CLINKERS

  • High energy content.
  • Essential for producing various types of cement.
  • Composed mainly of calcium silicates and aluminates.
  • Formed in rotary kilns at temperatures around 1,450°C.
  • Ground with gypsum to create the final cement product.
  • Provides the binding properties needed in concrete and mortar.

WHITE CLINKERS

  • Made from raw materials with low iron content.
  • Used in decorative and architectural concrete.
  • Provides high brightness and consistent color.
  • Requires precise temperature control during production.
  • Essential for producing smooth, white surfaces in construction.

SODIUM SULPHATE

Primary source of aluminium metal production, derived from aluminium oxide ores.

  • Key ingredient in detergents and paper production.
  • Sourced from natural deposits or as a by-product.
  • Soluble, used in drying processes.
  • Removes air bubbles in glass-making.
  • Known as "Glauber's salt" in medicine 4°.

CASTIC SODA

  • Key ingredient in soap and detergent production.
  • Used in paper and pulp processing.
  • Essential for water treatment and purification.
  • Acts as a strong cleaning agent in various industries.
  • Commonly applied in chemical manufacturing.

CASTIC POTASH

  • Key ingredient in soap and detergent production.
  • Used in chemical manufacturing and pH control.
  • Essential in biodiesel production.
  • Strong alkaline cleaner in various industries.
  • Commonly used in fertilizer formulations.
